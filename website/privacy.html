Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 31, 2026

🔒 Your privacy is our top priority. Pochi is designed to be 100% private by default.

Our Commitment to Privacy

At Pochi, we believe that financial tools should respect your privacy. We built Pochi with a "privacy-first" approach, meaning we collect the absolute minimum amount of data necessary to provide our service - which is essentially nothing.

🔒 What We DON'T Do

  • We do NOT collect any personal information
  • We do NOT track your browsing activity
  • We do NOT send data to external servers
  • We do NOT use cookies for tracking
  • We do NOT sell or share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT require account creation
  • We do NOT access your financial accounts

What Data We Store

Pochi stores only two types of data, and both are kept entirely on your local device:

1. Calculation History

Your recent fee calculations are stored locally in your browser's storage. This includes:

  • Provider name (e.g., "M-Pesa", "CRDB Bank")
  • Transaction amount
  • Calculated fee
  • Date/time of calculation

This data never leaves your device. You can clear it anytime from the settings, or enable auto-delete to remove it automatically after 24 hours, 7 days, or 30 days.

2. User Preferences

Your settings are stored locally, including:

  • Language preference (English/Swahili)
  • Theme preference (Light/Dark mode)
  • Auto-delete history setting
  • Impact unit preference
  • Tax breakdown toggle

How We Process Data

All calculations happen on your device. When you enter a transaction amount:

  1. The calculation is performed locally using fee data stored in the extension
  2. No data is sent to any server
  3. No network request is made for the calculation
  4. The result is displayed immediately on your screen

Fee Data Updates

Pochi includes fee data directly in the extension package. When we update fee structures (e.g., when M-Pesa changes their pricing), we release a new version of the extension. This means:

  • No automatic data fetching from external sources
  • No background network activity
  • You control when to update the extension

Third-Party Services

Pochi does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services. The only external links are:

  • Chrome Web Store (for installation/updates)
  • GitHub (for open-source code)
  • Support/donation links (only when you click them)

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible in the extension
  • Deletion: Clear history anytime from settings
  • Export: Your data is stored in standard browser storage
  • Opt-out: Enable "Never Save" to prevent history storage

Children's Privacy

Pochi does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The extension performs mathematical calculations only and does not require any personal information.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since Pochi does not collect contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes - please check this page periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Via GitHub Issues: github.com/Baberre11/Pochi
  • Via Feature Request Form on our website

💚 Thank you for trusting Pochi. We're committed to keeping your financial calculations private, secure, and completely under your control.
