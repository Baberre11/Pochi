Is Pochi free to use?

Yes, Pochi is 100% free. We don't charge anything, we don't show ads, and we don't ask for payments. It's a community project for Tanzania.

Is my data safe with Pochi?

Absolutely. Pochi is 100% private. All calculations happen on your device. We don't collect your name, phone number, or any personal information. Your calculation history is stored only on your computer.

Do I need to create an account?

No. Pochi works immediately after installation. No login, no registration, no phone number required. Just install and start calculating.

How accurate are the fees?

We update our fee data regularly from official provider sources. However, fees can change, so always double-check with your provider before sending large amounts.

Can I use Pochi on my phone?

Pochi is a browser extension for Chrome on desktop. For mobile, we recommend bookmarking our website (pochi.tz) to quickly check fees on your phone's browser.

Which providers are supported?

Currently: M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, CRDB Bank, NMB Bank, KCB Bank, Stanbic Bank, and Equity Bank. We're adding Airtel Money and HaloPesa soon.

Can I suggest a new feature?

Yes! Scroll down to the "Request a Feature" section and fill out the form. We read every suggestion and prioritize based on community needs.

Is Pochi open source?

Yes! Pochi is open source under the MIT License. Anyone can view the code, suggest improvements, or contribute. Visit our GitHub repository to learn more.
