We don't take your money. We don't track your data. We just show you the truth.

How to Use Pochi

1

Install the Extension

Download Pochi from the Chrome Web Store. Click "Add to Chrome" and the extension icon will appear in your browser toolbar.

Takes 30 seconds
↓
2

Click the Pochi Icon

Whenever you want to send money, click the green "P" icon in your browser toolbar. The popup opens instantly—no login required.

Instant
↓
3

Choose Your Provider

Select your provider from the dropdown: M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, CRDB, NMB, or any supported bank. Switch between Mobile Money and Bank tabs.

2 seconds
↓
4

Select Transaction Type

Pick what you're doing: Send Money, Pay Bill, or Withdraw. If sending, choose Registered or Unregistered recipient.

2 seconds
↓
5

Enter Amount

Type in how much you're sending. Use the quick buttons (5K, 10K, 50K, 100K) for faster entry. See the fee calculate instantly.

3 seconds
↓
6

See the Truth

View your exact fee in red. See what that fee could buy (bread, transport, data). Check if bank or mobile is cheaper. Decide wisely.

Now you know

Pro Tips

  • Compare Before Sending: Check both bank and mobile fees to find the cheapest option
  • Language Switch: Click "EN/SW" button anytime to switch between English and Swahili
  • Dark Mode: Open settings to switch to dark theme for night use
  • Auto-Delete: Set history to auto-delete after 24 hours, 7 days, or 30 days for privacy
  • Tax Breakdown: Enable in settings to see VAT (18%) and Excise Duty breakdown
