Why Use Pochi?

💰

Real-Time Fee Calculator

Instantly calculate fees for M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money, HaloPesa, and all major Tanzanian banks. Know exactly what you'll pay before you send.

🏦

Bank & Mobile Support

Compare fees across 7 mobile money providers and 5 major banks including CRDB, NMB, KCB, Stanbic, and Equity Bank.

The Problem We're Solving

In Tanzania, mobile money fees can reach up to 20% of small transactions. These fees are often hidden, unclear, and disproportionately affect low-income users who rely on mobile money for daily transactions.

Pochi brings transparency to this system. By showing exact fees, comparing with global standards, and highlighting what those fees could buy in daily life, we empower Tanzanians to make informed financial decisions.

High fees break trust. When you see money disappear, you return to cash. Cash excludes you from credit, savings, and growth. Transparency creates the demand for fair pricing.

The Hidden Tax on Trust
Every time you pay a fee without understanding it, you lose a little trust in the system.
Transparency → Demand → Lower Fees → Inclusion → Growth
